Susan B. Anthony

The Renegade Queen

Susan B. Anthony was most famous for voicing her opinion on women's rights.
Also, she wasn't afraid to take action. She was the main person to give women the right to vote.
She gave women all over the country a voice to pick who wanted to run their country.

Susan B. Anthony
  • 1.) She gave women the right to vote in the U.S.A
  • 2.) She spoke out against slavery.
  • 3.) She founded the National Woman Suffrage Association.
  • 4.) She was the leader of the woman suffrage movement in the beginning.
  • 5.) She became a feminist icon.
